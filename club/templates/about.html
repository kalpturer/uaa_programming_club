Uskudar American Academy Programming Club

The UAA Programming Club aims to teach the fundamentals of programming to its members. Throughout the year C and Python languages will be taught however the members are free to use whichever language they please in the club. As a club we will join many programming competitions including TUBITAK National Computer Science Olympiad, Google Code Jam, USACO and CCC but most importantly we will be organizing our very own contest CodeFest.

The club meets every Thursday after school.
